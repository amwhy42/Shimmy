How to Play

The Grid

The game board is a parallelogram filled with 24 equilateral triangles, each containing a letter. The ? is a wildcard — you choose its letter when you select it.

Building a Word

  • Tap any triangle to start your word.
  • Each next letter must be in a triangle that shares a point (vertex) with the last letter selected — not just an edge.
  • You cannot use the same triangle twice in one word.
  • Words can be 1 to 10 letters long.
  • Tap a triangle already in your word to backtrack to that point.

Submitting a Word

Tap ENTER WORD to submit. Words are checked against a dictionary. If valid, you score 1 point per letter.

Tap ✕ to clear your current selection.

Word Length Dots

The blue triangle (top-left) shows dots numbered 1–10. Each dot represents a word length. You can only score one word of each length per level — once a length is used, that dot turns green and that length is locked out.

The Wildcard ?

When you tap the ? triangle, a letter picker appears. Choose any letter — it acts as that letter for the current word. Your choice resets when you clear or submit.

Scoring & Levelling Up

  • Score 1 point per letter in each valid word.
  • Reach the NEXT LEVEL target to unlock LEVEL UP!.
  • Tap LEVEL UP! then tap one triangle to lock its letter permanently — it stays through all future reshuffles (shown in amber).
  • After levelling up, your score resets to 0 and all word lengths become available again with a fresh set of letters.
  • The target score increases by 1 each level.

Reachable Highlights

After selecting a letter, triangles you can move to next are subtly highlighted so you can plan your path.
