How to Play Shimmy Legacy

The Grid

24 triangles each hold a letter. The ? is a wildcard.

Selecting Letters

  • Tap a triangle to start your word.
  • Each next letter must share a vertex with the last.
  • Tap a selected letter to backtrack.
  • Words can be 1-10 letters long.

Scoring

  • Tap ENTER WORD to submit. Score 1 pt per letter.
  • Only one word of each length per level.
  • Reach NEXT LEVEL target to level up.

Levelling Up

Tap LEVEL UP! then tap a triangle to lock that letter permanently.
